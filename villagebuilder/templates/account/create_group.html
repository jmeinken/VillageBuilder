VillageBuilder Groups

VillageBuilder Groups make it easy to share items within your club, organization, etc. Simply create a group and invite people to join.

How it works:

  • If you create the group, you are the group owner.
  • All new members must be approved by the group owner before joining.
  • Whenever a member shares an item, they will have the option of sharing it with your entire group.
